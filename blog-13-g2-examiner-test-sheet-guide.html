The G2 Road Test Scoring Sheet – What Every Student Needs to Know

Published on July 23, 2024

DriveTest examiners use an examination sheet to score candidates during their road tests. While the G2 and G test sheets are similar, the G test sheet includes additional maneuvers for assessment, such as highway driving and since 2022 they've stopped testing for road-side stops, 3 point turns and parallel parking. Let’s walk through how the G2 scoring sheet works, what categories you’ll be marked on, and the top habits you should practice before your road test.

Final Thoughts

Making a mistake doesn’t always mean failure. What matters most is demonstrating safe, consistent habits across the full test. With practice in these categories, you’ll walk into your G2 exam confident and prepared.
