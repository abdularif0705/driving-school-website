About Us

Shifting Gears, Changing Lives

Founded in 2017 by Muhammad Arif Rajput and his son Abdul Rehman Arif, Rajput Driving School embodies the courage and resilience of the Rajput heritage of warriors. Our mission is to build confident and skilled drivers.

All of our instructors are certified by the Ministry of Transportation and bring years of practical experience. We cover everything from basic vehicle operation and traffic laws to advanced defensive driving techniques, including collision avoidance and emergency maneuvers Our patient and thorough approach ensures each student receives the attention they need.

Safety is our top priority. Our cars have dual brakes, ensuring instructors can guide students safely. We combine online classes with in-car lessons to build your skills and confidence. Upon completing our Beginner Driver Education (BDE) course you'll lower car insurance by up to $1000/year.

Whether you're a nervous beginner or an experienced driver looking to refine your skills, our patient instructors are here to help you succeed. With affordable rates, we provide exceptional value without compromising on quality.

We are proud of our 96% pass rate, a testament to the effectiveness of our teaching methods' and the dedication of our instructors. Our satisfied clients often praise our instructors for their patience, expertise, and ability to instill confidence.

Ready to start your journey to becoming a confident driver? Contact us today to schedule your first lesson!
