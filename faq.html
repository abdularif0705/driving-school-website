Our most popular course is the Beginner Driver Education (BDE) course, priced at $450. This comprehensive course includes 10 in-car lessons and 30 hours of online BDE instruction.

Enrolling in our Beginner's Drivers Education (BDE) package offers several benefits. You will reduce your car insurance upon completing our drivers ed course, lowering your car insurance by up to $1000. Our thorough training program equips you with the skills and confidence for safe driving. Additionally, completing the BDE course can expedite your G2 road test waiting time by 4 months, speeding up your path to becoming a fully licensed driver.
