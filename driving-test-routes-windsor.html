Driving Test Routes

Driving Test Routes Videos for Windsor and Chatham

At Rajput Driving School, we understand that taking a G2 & G driving test can be a nerve-wracking experience. Our goal is to help you feel confident and prepared. Below the text are some videos of the actual routes. But first here are a few tips to help you succeed:

  • Ensure you have all the necessary documents, including your learner's permit and identification.
  • Get plenty of practice driving in various conditions, such as during different times of the day and in different weather conditions.
  • Familiarize yourself with the test routes. Our driving test route videos provide a detailed look at what you can expect.
  • Stay calm and focused during the test. Remember to follow all traffic laws and signals.
  • Listen carefully to the examiner's instructions and ask for clarification if needed.
